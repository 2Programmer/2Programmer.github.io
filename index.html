Ralph Baer

Born on March, 8, 1922, Ralph .H. Baer was a German man who designed the first Working computer system game. The first console was the Odyssey Magnavox, He was known as "The Father of video Games." He won 4 awards and co-invented the game Simon he Sadly died in December, 6, 2014 and was married to Dena Whinston.
